About Age Calculator

What This Calculator Does

CalcMyAge.org provides free, accurate age calculation tools used by over 100,000 visitors monthly. Our suite of 7 calculators helps you:

  • Age Calculator - Find your exact age in years, months, days, hours, and minutes
  • Age on Date - Calculate your age on any past or future date
  • Age Difference - Find the exact age gap between two people
  • Days Old - Discover how many days you've been alive
  • Life Expectancy - Estimate your lifespan based on WHO data
  • Korean Age - Calculate your age in the traditional East Asian system
  • Chronological Age - Years:Months format for Pearson assessments and educational evaluations

How We Calculate Age

Our calculators use precise date mathematics following international standards:

  • Western/International Age: Age increases on each birthday anniversary. We calculate the exact difference between dates accounting for varying month lengths (28-31 days) and leap years (every 4 years, except century years not divisible by 400).
  • Korean Age (Traditional): Following the traditional Korean age system, everyone is 1 at birth and gains a year on New Year's Day. Note: South Korea officially adopted international age in June 2023.
  • Chronological Age: Per APA testing standards, we provide Years:Months format (e.g., 7:4) with decimal age for psychological and educational assessments.
  • Life Expectancy: Estimates based on WHO Global Health Observatory country-specific data, adjusted for gender.

Accuracy & Limitations

What's accurate:

  • Age calculations are accurate to the second using JavaScript's Date object
  • Leap years are handled correctly (Gregorian calendar rules)
  • Month lengths are calculated precisely (not approximated as 30 days)
  • Time zone is based on your browser's local settings

Limitations to note:

  • Life expectancy estimates are statistical averages, not individual predictions. They should not replace medical advice.
  • Historical dates before 1582 (pre-Gregorian calendar) may have discrepancies
  • Korean age calculator reflects traditional system; official South Korean documents now use international age
