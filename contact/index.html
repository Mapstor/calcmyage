First, double-check that you entered the correct birthdate in the right format (month/day/year for US format). If you're using the Chronological Age Calculator, make sure you entered both the birthdate AND the test date. If the calculation still seems wrong, please contact us with: (1) the birthdate you entered, (2) the result you got, and (3) what you expected.

Our Korean Age Calculator uses the traditional system where you're 1 at birth and gain a year on January 1. Note that South Korea officially switched to international age in June 2023, so official documents there now use the same age system as Western countries.

Our calculators provide accurate results suitable for planning and reference. However, for official purposes (legal documents, medical records, standardized testing), always verify with the relevant authority or official system. Our Chronological Age Calculator follows Pearson assessment standards.

Our Life Expectancy Calculator uses WHO data for population averages. These are statistical estimates, not personal predictions. Individual lifespans depend on genetics, lifestyle, healthcare access, and many other factors. This tool is for informational purposes only.
