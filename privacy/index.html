Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Privacy at a Glance

Key Points
  • Your birthdate stays in your browser - All calculations happen client-side using JavaScript
  • No personal data is transmitted - We never send your dates to our servers
  • No account required - Use all calculators anonymously
  • No cookies for tracking - We only use essential cookies if any
  • No data sold - We never sell personal information to third parties

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

CalcMyAge.org is designed with privacy in mind. We specifically do not collect:

  • Your date of birth or any dates you enter into our calculators
  • Your name, email address, or contact information (unless you voluntarily contact us)
  • Your IP address for personal identification
  • Your precise location or GPS coordinates
  • Health information or medical data
  • Financial information

2. How Our Calculators Work

When you use any calculator on CalcMyAge.org:

  1. You enter a date into the form field in your browser
  2. JavaScript code running in your browser performs the calculation
  3. The result is displayed on your screen
  4. Your data never leaves your device

This is called client-side processing. The calculations happen entirely within your web browser, not on our servers. If you close the browser tab, the data is gone - we never had it.

3. Information We May Collect

We may collect limited, anonymous information for website operation:

Analytics (Anonymous)

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This may include:

  • Pages visited (e.g., "age calculator page")
  • General geographic region (country level, not precise location)
  • Device type (mobile, desktop)
  • Referral source (how you found us)

This data is aggregated and cannot identify individual users.

Contact Form Submissions

If you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name (as you provide it)
  • Your email address (to respond to you)
  • Your message content

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

Server Logs

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically log:

  • IP addresses (typically truncated/anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Date and time of access
  • Referring URL

These logs are used for security, debugging, and aggregate statistics only.

4. Cookies

We use minimal or no cookies:

  • Essential cookies: May be used for basic site functionality (e.g., remembering your theme preference)
  • Analytics cookies: If used, are privacy-respecting and do not track individuals across sites
  • No advertising cookies: We do not use cookies for targeted advertising
  • No third-party tracking: We do not embed social media trackers or cross-site tracking pixels

You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting calculator functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We select services that respect user privacy.

6. Data Retention

  • Calculator inputs: Not retained (never sent to our servers)
  • Contact form submissions: Retained until your inquiry is resolved, then deleted within 90 days
  • Server logs: Automatically purged after 30 days
  • Analytics data: Aggregated data retained for up to 24 months

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under data protection laws such as GDPR (EU) or CCPA (California):

  • Right to access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to object: Object to certain processing of your data
  • Right to portability: Receive your data in a portable format

Since we collect minimal data, these requests are typically simple. Contact us to exercise any of these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Our calculators are general-purpose tools suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information through our contact form, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

CalcMyAge.org is hosted in the United States. If you access our site from outside the US, be aware that any information you provide through the contact form may be transferred to the US. By using our contact form, you consent to this transfer. Note that our calculators do not transmit data, so there is no international transfer of calculator inputs.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Material changes will be highlighted. Your continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: info@calcmyage.org
  • Contact Form: calcmyage.org/contact

We aim to respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
